This Website

This website is a static informational site. We do not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies, and we do not ask you to provide any personal data. However, two third-party services involved in delivering the site may process your IP address, which is considered personal data under the GDPR.

Hosting — GitHub Pages

The website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. (a subsidiary of Microsoft Corporation), United States. When you visit the site, GitHub's servers automatically process technical connection data, including your IP address, in server log files for the purpose of delivering the content and ensuring security and stability. This processing is based on our legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in providing a secure and functioning website. See GitHub's privacy statement: GitHub Privacy Statement.

Fonts — Google Fonts

This website loads web fonts (Sora, Fraunces, Noto Serif JP) from Google Fonts, a service provided by Google LLC, United States. To deliver these fonts, your browser connects to Google servers (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com), which transmits your IP address to Google. This processing is based on our legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in presenting the site consistently. For more information see the Google Privacy Policy and Google Fonts & Privacy.

GitHub and Google are based in the United States and may process the technical data described above on servers located outside the European Economic Area.
